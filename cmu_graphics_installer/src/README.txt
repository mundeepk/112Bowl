Project Title and Description:
Welcome to 112 Bowl!

Inspired by the mobile arcade game RetroBowl, 112 Bowl is my version of the game with a 112 spin. In 112 Bowl, you are playing against another "team," and your goal is to reach 112 points to win! 112 Bowl is a high-scoring, action-packed game. 

Run Instructions:
No libraries or modules needs to be installed, outside of CMU graphics. 

Shortcut commands:
press 'g' to simulate a field goal
press 'r' if you want to restart
press 't' to simulate a defender tackling you
